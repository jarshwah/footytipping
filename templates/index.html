Rules Of The Game!

28th Mar 2011

So it occurred to me that, maybe, not every one knows all the rules. I didn't. Rob and I spent some time discussing them and have come up with the following.

I forgot to Tip!

Don't worry too much. If you forget to put your tips in one week, we'll calculate your score as if you selected all the away teams. The away teams are usually at a disadvantage, so make sure to get your tips in on time so you aren't disadvantaged with them!

Draws!

I hadn't considered what would happen if there was a draw until it happened in the very first round! All people receive a point for a draw, since it's not possible to choose a draw in this competition. Everyone wins. No one is eligible for clean sweep points if there is a draw however.

Clean Sweep!

If you happen to tip all matches in a round correctly (draws don't count!), you will receive 1 extra point for that round. This is our version of a weekly prize. If you forgot to tip and all the away teams happen to win, you won't receive clean sweep bonuses either.

When does tipping close?

You can tip a match right up until the game begins. That means you can do all your tips on a Wednesday, change your mind on Saturday about a game the following day, and change your tip. You can not vote for a game that has already begun. The start and end times of a round don't matter, it's all about when the game starts.

Scoring!

  • 1 point for a winning tip
  • 0 points for a losing tip
  • 1 point for a draw, regardless of tip
  • 1 extra point for a 'clean sweep' (see above)

Leader Board

The ranking on the leader board is purely based on number of tips. If two people share the same number, the order displayed is order of signup. That doesn't matter. We'll use a complicated method of rock, scissors, paper for a tie break (or something) should it be needed at the end of the season.
